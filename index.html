About Me

My name is Jungmin Ahn, a Masters of Engineering student studying Operations Research and Information Engineering, specifically in the Data Analytics concentration. Prior to coming here, I studied Industrial and Systems Engineering at the University of Washington's Seattle campus, where I took the Data Science track and took part in the Research Honors program.

I have always had a strong interest in mathematics and computer programs, so discovering the rise of big data in today's world was an integral part of my choice of pursuing Data Science and Analytics. I am a keen learner, currently specializing in using statistical programming and querying languages and software such as Python, SQL and R to develop visualizations and models to be able to translate intimidating datasets with millions of rows into stories that anyone can read and learn from.

I am honored to have worked with many incredible faculty members and teammates in countless projects over my academic career, which have helped me gain a great amount of understanding in how wide the scope of data analytics is and why it is such a powerful, popular skill to master in today's world.

Besides constantly working on my analytical programming skills every day, I also enjoy watching sports (particularly the NFL, MLB, and European soccer), reading about sports and playing table tennis. My goal is to always be a better person than I was yesterday, every day!
